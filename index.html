    VSAT For ATM machine Internet Conection

    VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) is a station receiving signals from satellites with a dish-shaped receiver antenna with a diameter of less than three meters. The main function of the VSAT is to receive and send data to the satellite. In the VSAT installation project for an Mandiri ATM machine, I was in charge of installing the antenna, configuration modem for pointing and crosspole the antenna so that it was line of sigh with the satellite. And finally do a test the ATM machine.

    VSAT is not only for ATM machines but can also be used for internet, radar and other satellite-related

    One example of the VSAT communication system is from VSAT to satellite to the intended satellite station then back to the satellite and then from the satellite to send data to the VSAT
